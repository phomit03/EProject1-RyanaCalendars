The Birth of the French Calendar

The calendar is considered a system of organizing time recording. It displays the regulation of civil life religious ceremonies. The calendar is also used for historical and archaeological purposes.

The ancients relied on the movement of the sun and moon. From there they calculated the agricultural calendar for example a year with 356 days. These days will be divided equally into 12 months and then divided into full hours in season.

The birth of the French calendar has the effect of helping to plant the right seasonally.


The Birth of Astronomy



Astronomy is one of the earliest sciences. Humans also rely on the movement of the sun and moon to find the rules. The most basic findings of prehistoric people are the recognition of the times of the changing seasons. In addition, the ancients believed that mysterious astronomical phenomena would be a harbinger in life and strengthen the beliefs of mankind.

In particular, astronomy has a very important application in cultivation and breeding. The farmer a copper that hunters observe and astronomically to recognize the seasonal catch as well as production accordingly.

By ancient times, mankind appeared ancient civilizations such as Egyptian civilization. Egyptian civilization made great strides When inventing the lunar calendar with 12 months. There will be 29 to 30 days per month. And every 2 and 3 years they will add one month to fit the seasons of the year.


Application of French Calendar and Astronomy
